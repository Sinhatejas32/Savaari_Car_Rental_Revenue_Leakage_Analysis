# Savaari_Car_Rental_Revenue_Leakage_Analysis
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze and monitor key business metrics for Savaari Car Rentals, transforming raw booking data into actionable insights for strategic decision-making.The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of operational performance through KPIs such as Total Revenue, Actual Bookings, Net Orders, Cancellation Rate, Revenue Loss, and Customer Booking Trends.

Performed data cleaning, transformation, and modeling using Power Query and DAX to create dynamic measures and business-focused visualizations. Conducted exploratory data analysis to identify booking patterns, seasonal demand fluctuations, customer preferences, platform performance, and cancellation behavior across multiple dimensions.

Designed interactive dashboards with drill-through functionality, slicers, and filters to enable detailed analysis by city, platform type, vehicle category, and booking status. The analysis revealed high-performing regions, premium vehicle demand trends, and revenue leakage caused by cancellations, helping uncover opportunities for operational optimization and revenue growth.

This project demonstrates expertise in business analytics, KPI reporting, data storytelling, dashboard design, and translating complex datasets into meaningful business insights.

**Tools Used**: **Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Data Modeling, Data Visualization, Business Analytics.**
